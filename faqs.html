FAQ

  • Girls Connect is a campaign that supports girls - it is a phone line that combines the power of storytelling with one-to-one support.

    The stories are all about the issues girls face while growing up in Nigeria and designed to make girls feel more confident. After listening to the story, girls can have a real life conversation with female role models who can answer questions from the stories.

  • Girls dial the short code above and listen to pre-recorded stories containing inspiring, entertaining and educational lessons about the lives of four characters. These four characters face every day challenges involving, safety, earning money, education and health.

    Once the story is finished, girls are connected to a ‘role model’: specially-trained females who can answer girls’ questions truthfully and talk through the stories they have listened to.

  • Girls Connect is run by the organisation Girl Effect Nigeria, in partnership with iSON.

  • If you have any questions about Girls Connect please e-mail nigeria@girleffect.org
